Is Exactly: Your exact spelling, plus JewishGen's built-in name synonyms. The only mode that reads wildcards.

Wildcards (*): Fill in unknown letters anywhere in a name. Works only with Is Exactly.

Comma = OR, Space = AND: A comma finds either name; a space requires both.
