Background

The Makofski collection centers on the work of David Makofski who as Chair of the Leeds Jewish Refugee Committee set up the Trainee Scheme.  This scheme aimed to help young, Jewish refugee men find work in Leeds in 1938-1939.

This information comes from the 3 trainee books which records key details about these men.  Records in addition to the ones added to the searchable database exist at the West Yorkshire Archive Service (see below acknowledgements).  Only those records for individuals 100 years after their birthdate were added to this database.

Database

This database includes 238 survivors.  The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Nationality
  • Date Arrived in England or Record Entry Date
  • Comments
  • Record Reference #
  • Record Finding #
