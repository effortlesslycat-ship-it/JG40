Background

There were a large number of small transports of Jews (over 6,500 persons) from Kraków to various other locations in Poland during the period November 29, 1940 through April 2, 1941.  The material does not include information on the ultimate fate of the persons.

Database

This database includes 6,701 transported Jews listed on 234 images.

The fields of the database are as follows:

  • Transport Number
  • Destination
  • Departure Date
  • Departure Place
  • District
  • Number
  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Maiden Surname
  • Marital Status
  • Date of Birth
  • Last Residence - (Kraków or other town)
  • Last Address
  • Comments

The following will help the researcher translate terms within the Marital Status field:

     German      English
     Geschieden      Divorced
     Verheiratet      Married
     Ledig      Single
     Witwe      Widow
