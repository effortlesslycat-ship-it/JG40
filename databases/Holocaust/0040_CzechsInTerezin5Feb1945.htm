Czech Inmates at Bergen Belsen and Theresienstadt

Czechoslovak People Who, According to Information Received from Persons Released to Switzerland (from Terezín), were still in Terezín on February 5, 1945

List originally appeared in Bulletin #14 of Czechoslovak Jewish Committee

INTRODUCTION

This dataset contain the names of 383 Czechoslovak inmates at Bergen Belsen and Theresienstadt (Terezín), who, according to information received from persons released to Switzerland (from Terezín), were still in Terezín on February 5, 1945.

The fields included in this list are as follows:

  • Last Name
  • First Name
  • Additional Facts
  • Town/Country of Origin
  • Status

BIBLIOGRAPHY

  • Czechoslovak Jewish Committee, Bulletin #14, May-June 1945, published in New York.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

Special acknowledgment to Barb Heinrich, who contributed these lists with this comment: "My mother-in-law had received a copy [of the Bulletin] when she was searching for her parents.  Both are listed in the bulletin.  We found it after she died when we went through her belongings."  She donated these lists to JewishGen with the hope "that someone finds important information about relatives in these lists."
