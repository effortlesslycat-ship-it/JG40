The Auschwitz Project on Geni

The Auschwitz Project [https://www.geni.com/projects/Auschwitz-Concentration-Camp/11003] on Geni.com [https://www.geni.com] seeks to collect profiles for all of the people imprisoned by the Nazis in the Auschwitz Concentration Camp in Poland. To date, over 64,016 profiles have been added to the project. Results on JewishGen include links to the profiles and their family trees on Geni.com.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
