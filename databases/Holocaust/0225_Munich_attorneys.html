This database contains the names of 194 attorneys living in Munich during the 1930s.

Background

This is a short collection of Jewish attorneys resident in Munich at an unspecified time in the 1930s.

The cross by some names indicates that these persons received “Passierscheine” (permits), but there is no indication what these permits meant.  Little else is know about the names on this list.

Database

This database includes 194 records of attorneys with various degrees living in Munich.  The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Title

Acknowledgments

The information contained in this database was indexed from the files of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (RG 14.053M).  The original source material is from the Museum’s Stuttgart, Germany collection.
