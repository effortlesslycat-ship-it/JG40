Jews living in Vienna during the Nazi occupation (1938-1945)

BACKGROUND

This data set includes a listing of Jews living in Vienna during the Nazi occupation from 1938-1945.  This is not a complete list of all Jews, but rather a list compiled in 1945, a copy of which is held by the Israel National Library's Central Archives of the History of the Jewish People in Jerusalem, file AU-99.

DATABASE

This database consists of 1,365 Jews living in Vienna under Nazi occupation.

The fields in the database are:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Street Address
  • District Number
  • Comments

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

The original source of the material can be found in the Israel National Library's Central Archives of the History of the Jewish People in Jerusalem, file AU-99.  Yehuda Rubin, a JewishGen volunteer, did the data entry to create the database.
