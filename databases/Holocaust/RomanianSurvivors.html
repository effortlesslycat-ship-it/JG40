Survivors Returning to Sighet and Maramures

7,281 Records

Holocaust · Survivors · Maramures · Romania

Background

There are two record books in this set: Book I, Survivors Returning to Sighet, Maramures, Romania, and Book II, Survivors Returning to Other Towns in Maramures, Romania. These books have been kept in the Jewish Community Center in Sighet. They are not in the Romanian National Archives as far as is known, and the identity of the compilers is uncertain, though the books were also used to track monetary and goods disbursements.

There are a grand total of 7,281 records: 2,133 for those returning to Sighet, and 5,148 for those returning to various towns in Maramures.
