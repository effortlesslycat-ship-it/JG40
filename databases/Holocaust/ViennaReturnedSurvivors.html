List of Austrian Holocaust Survivors Returned to Vienna

Background

A list Austrian Jews returned to Vienna from various concentration camps.The collection is held at the Central Archives of the History of the Jewish People in Jerusalem, Israel.

As of December 2020, there are 2,203 records in this collection.

Work is ongoing. To volunteer as a transcriber, please contact Randy Schoenberg (Director of Austria-Czech Research) at rschoenberg@JewishGen.org.
