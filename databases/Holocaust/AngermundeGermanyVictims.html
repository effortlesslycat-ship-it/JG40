Angermünde, Germany Victims

BACKGROUND

This is a small set of 43 victims of the Holocaust from the town of Angermünde.  Angermünde is a town in the district of Uckermark in the state of Brandenburg, Germany. It is about 43 miles (69 km) northeast of Berlin.  The city prepared the list during the 70th anniversary of the end of World War II.  The city prepared the list with data they accumulated from Yad Vashem.

DATABASE

This database consists of 43 Individuals from the town of Angermünde, Germany.

The fields in the database are:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Maiden Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth or Residence
  • Deportation / Evacuation Date
  • Deported From
  • Evacuated To
  • Date of Death
  • Place of Death
  • Comments
