Bucharest Pogrom Victims - January 22-23, 1941

BACKGROUND

This dataset contains the names of 117 Jews who died in the Bucharest pogrom organized by the Iron Guards, with the aid of the Nazis, on January 22-23, 1941. The victim list appeared in a news article in the "Boston Jewish Advocate" on August 29, 1941. It was gathered by the United Roumanian Jews of America, seeking information on the fate of their families. The list was then re-printed in ROM-SIG News, Volume 9, Spring 2001, and was transcribed from there.

The list was originally submitted for the 2001 newsletter by David Rosen and was transcribed for this upload by Carolyn Halbert.

DATABASE

The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given name
  • Death Date
  • Death Place
  • Notes
