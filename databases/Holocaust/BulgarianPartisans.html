The Jeff Malka Sephardic Collection

Jewish Resistance in World War II Bulgaria:
An Introduction and Reference Materials

By Michael L. Hoffman

Background

Michael L. Hoffman, Ph.D., currently residing in Sofia, Bulgaria has graciously made his article about the Jewish Bulgarian Partisans available to all. In addition to being of general historical interest, it is also of genealogical interest as it includes a list of 272 Jewish Bulgarian Partisans. There is also a large bibliography on the Bulgarian resistance with both English and annotated Bulgarian sources.

The entire article can be downloaded by clicking here.

Database

This database consists of 272 Sephardic Jewish partisans from Bulgaria. The database was created from Annex D of the article mentioned above (Ghetto Fighters House; Grabcheva, 1984; Kohen; Konsistoriya Tsentralna na Evreite v Bulgariia, 1958. Partisan names are shown in parentheses).

The fields in the database are:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Origin
  • Date of Death
