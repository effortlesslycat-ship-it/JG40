ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS

Special acknowledgment to Barb Heinrich, who contributed these lists with this comment: "My mother-in-law had received a copy [of the Bulletin] when she was searching for her parents. She donated these lists to JewishGen with the hope "that someone finds important information about relatives in these lists."
