Jewish Holocaust Survivors Located in Venice - June 30, 1945

Background

At the end of WWII, survivors had to register in the towns where they were located. This is a list of 118 non-Italian survivors located in Venice. The survivors came from various countries, primarily Austria, Poland and Yugoslavia.

The information consists of name and year and place of birth, where given. Local address also appears, but has not been entered.

Database

This database includes 118 survivors. The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Maiden Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Town of Birth
  • Nationality

Acknowledgments
