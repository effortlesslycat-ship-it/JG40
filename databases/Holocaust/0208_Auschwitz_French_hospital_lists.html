Background

This database includes 333 records from various lists collected by the French government at Auschwitz.

At the end of WWII the French Government sent teams to former concentration camps to try to obtain information on the fate of French citizens/deportees who had not survived/returned.  In the process of conducting these investigations they often collected documents which not only had French data but also information relating to other prisoners.

This reel is part of a larger collection of material collected at the International Tracing Service in Bad Arolsen dealing with Auschwitz.  It primarily consists of Auschwitz material such as transport lists but also includes information on survivors who were found in Auschwitz at liberation.  These lists are part of the material collected by French Government inquiry teams sent to Auschwitz to attempt to ascertain the fate of French nationals.  Copies of this material were provided to ITS.

The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Prisoner #
  • Place of Birth
  • Date of Birth
  • Date Arrived
  • Date of Death
  • Source (see above table)
