Inmates of Theresienstadt Ghetto

BACKGROUND

This dataset contains an index of Geni.com profiles of persons who were inmates of the ghetto Theresienstadt also referred to as Theresienstadt Ghetto located in what is now the Czech Republic. There are 41,665 records in this dataset, each of which links to the profile that has been created on Geni.com.

The fields in the database include:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Location of Birth
  • Date of Death
  • Location of Death
  • In some instances, Burial & Address information
