Mohel Book of Dr. Herz Herzl, Lackenbach, Austria

Background

This database contains 903 records from the Mohel book of Dr. Herz Herzl of Lackenbach, Austria, 1821-1869. The information comes from the Central Archives of the History of the Jewish People in Jerusalem, AU-261.

For additional information, see: https://www.dropbox.com/sh/u72ls5ll77q45oh/AAD7Mm6CD-GcGdSM0SwQ-6BSa?dl=0

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.

Searching the Database

This database can be searched via the JewishGen Austria-Czech Database or the JewishGen Unified Search
