Index of Prague Taxpayers - 1782

Background

This collection includes index cards created by Leon Ruzicka (1866-1931) of taxpayer records for Jews from Prague in 1782. The index cards are at the Wiener Stadt- und Landesarchiv (MA 8) in Vienna, and the tax records are held by the Österreichische Staatsarchiv in Vienna. Scans of the index cards are at https://www.dropbox.com/sh/65runavsitssenz/AAA-9f_ggfEq8Q_hv6Dw4n7Ca?dl=0. There are a a total of 1,251 records in the collection.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
