Converts from Judaism: 1915-1945

This database contains an index of more than 27,000 people who converted from Judaism between 1915-1945. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • Number
  • Date
  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Birth Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • District
  • Country
  • Marital Status
  • Occupation
  • Vienna District
  • Street
  • Number
  • Religion (not always indicated)

For further information, contact: E Randol Schoenberg, Director of Austria-Czech Research.

Searching the Database

This database can be searched via the JewishGen Austria-Czech Database or the JewishGen Unified Search
