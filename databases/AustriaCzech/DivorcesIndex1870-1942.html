Divorces/Scheidungs Index: 1870-1942

This database contains an index of 10,276 divorce records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • Year
  • Number
  • Date
  • Husband Surname
  • Husband Given Name
  • Husband Place of Birth
  • Wife Surname
  • Wife Given Name
  • Wife Place of Birth
  • Place of Marriage
  • Date of Marriage
  • Children

For further information, contact: E Randol Schoenberg, Director of Austria-Czech Research.

Searching the Database

This database can be searched via the JewishGen Austria-Czech Database or the JewishGen Unified Search
