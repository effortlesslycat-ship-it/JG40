Death registers from Achab Haidler and Chewra

Background

Achab Haidler transcribed death registers from a number of towns in connection with his work indexing numerous Jewish cemeteries (see www.chewra.com). For the ones where the particular registers are identifiable, I have added a link to the register book.

There are 8,668 records in this collection.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
