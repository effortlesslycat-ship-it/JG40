Prague Marriage Licenses 1734-1739

This database contains an index of 320 marriage licenses from Prague, 1734-1739, from the Czech State Archives and the Prague Municipal Archives. The spelling is often very unclear and awkward. Names are transcribed as well as they can be deciphered.

For further information, or to obtain access to scans of the orginal documents, contact: E Randol Schoenberg, Director of Austria-Czech Research.

Searching the Database

This database can be searched via the JewishGen Austria-Czech Database or the JewishGen Unified Search
