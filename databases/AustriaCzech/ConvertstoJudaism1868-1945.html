Converts to Judaism: 1868-1945

This database contains an index of 14,349 people who converted to Judaism between 1868-1948. Most of the records include the individuals first and last name, and year of conversion. Some also include the date the conversion took place, the convert's age, birthplace, and additional notes.

For further information, contact: E Randol Schoenberg, Director of Austria-Czech Research.

Searching the Database

This database can be searched via the JewishGen Austria-Czech Database or the JewishGen Unified Search
