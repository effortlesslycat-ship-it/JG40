Jewish Deportations from Austria

This database contains an index of 62,279 records of Jews deported from Austria to various concentration camps. Records from the Documentation Centre of Austrian Resistance. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • Serial Number
  • DOW-ID
  • Surname
  • First Name
  • Birth Date
  • Birth Surname (if Different)
  • Place of Birth
  • Last Address
  • Vienna District#
  • Street
  • Deportation Date
  • Place Deported From
  • Place Deported To
  • Date of Death
  • Place of Death
  • Transport 1
  • Transport 1#
  • Transport 2
  • Transport 2#
  • Comment
  • Jewish Property Office
  • Gender

For further information, contact: E Randol Schoenberg, Director of Austria-Czech Research.
