Turkish Community of Vienna
1788-1818

N.M.Gelber wrote an article entitled “Contribution a l’histoire des Juifs espagnols de Vienne” [Contribution to the history of Spanish Jews in Vienna] and published in Revue des Etudes Juives, 1934, 97:114-151 and 98:44-49. He listed there the first members of Vienna Turkish community that counted 126 members and their families – a total of some 360 souls – between the years 1788-1818.

For more details on the Vienna Turkish community see the introductions to the Birth and Weddings Databases found on the following sites:
