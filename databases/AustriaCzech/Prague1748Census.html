Prague: Index of the 1792-1794 Census

Background

Index of the Prague Jewish Census for 1792 and 1794.

The census is viewable as a pdf from http://pvh.ff.cuni.cz/soupisy.htm. Page numbers are for the published volume, Soupis židovských rodin v Čechách z roku 1793. 6,1. Praha - 1792, Praha - 1794 : ed.: Ivana Ebelová, available at https://www.amazon.com/Soupis-Zidovskych-Rodin-Cechach-Praha/dp/8086712346.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
