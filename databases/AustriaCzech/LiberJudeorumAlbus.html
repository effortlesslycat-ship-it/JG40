Liber Judeorum Albus

The Municipal Archive of the City of Prague holds a set of books called Knihy zidovske bile (Libri albi Judeorum).  The Liber Judeorum Albus, or Jewish White Book, was a book used to record real property and mortgage transactions among the Jewish community in Prague.  The earliest books cover the dates 1577-1619 and 1601-1701.  Subsequent books begin after 1668 and continue at least through 1804.  JewishGen has access to the first 40 books and the indices for the next 4.  The books can be accessed at https://zayt.org/collections/pragapublica or from the Prague Municipal Archive website http://katalog.ahmp.cz/pragapublica/  (In Municipal Chronicles Search "Liber Judeorum" or "Zidovska kniha bila" or "Kniha nalezu").
