Lackenbach Mohel Book by Rabbi Neufeld

Background

This database contains information from the Mohel book for the town of Lackenbach, Austria and the surrounding region of Burgenland by Rabbi Neufeld. The data set consists of 345 entries from 1882 through 1924. Thanks to Yohanan Loeffler for inputting the data.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
