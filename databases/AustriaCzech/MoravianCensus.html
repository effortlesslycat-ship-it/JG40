Moravian Census

Background

The Moravian Provincial Archives in Brno contains various local town census or conscription record books from the years 1857 through 1921 that pertain to the formerly designated Jewish areas of certain Moravian towns.  A number of these "Jewish town" books can be found at https://www.mza.cz/scitacioperaty/digisada/search by searching "Zidovska." Although not all of the residents were Jewish at the time these records were made, JewishGen has indexed them for this database.
