Knopfelmacher Bris Book

Background

The bris record book of the mohel Jakob Knopflmacher (1834-1880) who was active in Austria, Moravia, Slovakia and Hungary. The births occurred 1853-1868.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
