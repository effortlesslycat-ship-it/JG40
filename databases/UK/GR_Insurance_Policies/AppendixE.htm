Guildhall Library reference are Ms. 8752, two volumes and Ms. 8753 also in two volumes.  They have been rearranged in alphabetical order in the names of the Merchants shipping.
Only the policies in Ms. 8752 have a numerical reference. There is a gap of 20 years between the two pairs. Three of the shippers, Coysigarne, de la Fontaine and Rigail may be well Hugenot rather than Jewish.
