  • Bevis Marks Marriage Index, 1836-1901 (1,253 records)
    These records from the books of the Spanish & Portuguese Jews' Congregation, contain limited data. Each records contain names of bridge and bridegroom and the year in which the marriage took place.  Detailed Information may be obtained from the Chief Archivist for a small fee.
    /jcr-uk/London/lauderdale/index.htm & /jcr-uk/London/city_bevis/index.htm

  • Princes Street (Princelet Street) Synagogue, 1897-1907 (100 records)
    These records were transcribed by Louise Messik with the consent of the Tower Hamlets Local History Library and Archives from their holding of the marriage certificates. http://jewishgen.org/jcr-uk/London/EE_princelet/index.htm

  • Sandys Row Synagogue, 1894-1930 (800+ records)
    These records were transcribed by Gina Marks from the original marriage books of the Synagogue.
    http://jewishgen.org/jcr-uk/London/EE_sandys/index.htm

  • West London Synagogue, 1842-1981 (4,933 records)
    West London Synagogue made available to Louise Messik all their birth, marriage and death original registers which are archived at the Hartley Institute, Southampton University for transcription purposes.
