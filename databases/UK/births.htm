West London Synagogue of British Jews, Birth Register 1844-1905 (859 records)©

Birth Register 1 of the West London Synagogue of British Jews, London, who brought all their original Birth, Marriage and Burial Registers from their archive at the Hartley Institute, Southampton University and made them available to Louise Messik for transcription purposes in order that they would be of genealogical research use. All the WLS records were transcribed by Louise Messik
