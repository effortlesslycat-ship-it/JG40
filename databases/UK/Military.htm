UNITED KINGDOM
MILITARY RECORDS (JEWISH)


Additions to the Roll of Honour 1914-1921 (425 records)

Additions to those whose names are published at http://www.roll-of-honour.com/. They have been researched and collated by Harold Pollins and contain Military number, unit or regiment, names, dates of death, ages at death, dates of birth,  next of kin, cemetery or memorial, synagogue membership and any comments of the researcher 
Brothers who Died in Service WWII (55 records)

Researched and collated by Harold Pollins.  The records carry the names, addresses, rank, unit or regiment, age at death, date of death, next of kin, cemetery or memorial and any comments of the researcher  

Boer War 1899-1902 (2,075 records)

Researched by a JGSGB member who remains anonymous these records carry the names, rank, unit or regiment of soldiers of the Boer War mentioned in the Jewish Chronicle 
Grimsby Soldiers WWI (43 records)

Researched and collated by Harold Pollins.  The records carry the names, rank, unit or regiment, date of death, parents and any comments of the researcher  
Jewish Officers in the Army, Navy and Territorial Forces 1912 (186 records) 


Researched and collated by Harold Pollins. The records  carry the name, rank, unit or regiment and any comments of the researcher, as well as the date of death and names of parents of fallen soldiers."
