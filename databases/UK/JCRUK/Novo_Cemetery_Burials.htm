BEVIS MARKS BURIAL REGISTER - NOVO CEMETERY 1733 - 1913
 © Nigel Fidlan 2008


Please note the following when searching this database

  • It was and remains customary for some Sephardi surnames to contain a first and second surname.  In this database the first surname has been incorporated into the given name in capital letters

  • Many of the deceased's first names were not entered in the register and thus you will find 'no name given' alongside the first surname

  • The comment column may include an observation such as son, daughter, wife, widow etc., or stillborn (up to five weeks old)

  • There are a couple of burials of a limb only

JGSGB and JCR-UK are grateful to Nigel Fidlan for undertaking the task of transcribing this data from the Bevis Marks Records Part VI.  This was undertaken with consent from the Spanish and Portuguese Jews Congregation.
