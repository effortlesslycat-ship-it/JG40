Pruzhany District Records from 1795 to 1958

These records were originally created by members of the Pruzhany district Jewish diaspora in 1999. These included Herbert J. Maletz of Staten Island, N.Y., Jose Serlin of Buenos Aires, Argentina, and Jay Lenefsky of Israel. Many other people helped and contributed material to the site, including Herbert Maletz's brother Leonard Maletz of Massachusetts and Florida (who died 18 June 2009), and David Waldshan.

The Pruzhany district includes the major towns of Bereza, Malech, Pruzhany, Selets, and Shereshevo.
