This database contains 3,043 records of conscripts in Brest and Pinsk districts of Polesie province of Poland (now Belarus), primarily from 1928 to 1939.  The birthdates range from 1891 to 1918 for Brest district, and from 1905 to 1912 for Pinsk district.

Database Fields:

The database contains the following columns of information:

  • Name:  Surname (last name, family name) + First name(s) (given names) of the conscript.
  • Father:  Father’s given name.
  • Residence:  Town of residence of the conscript.
  • Birth Year:  The conscript's year of birth.
  • District:  Either 'Brest' or 'Pinsk'.
  • Description:  Title of the list in which the information was found.
