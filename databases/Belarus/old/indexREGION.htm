All Belarus Database

The "All Belarus Database" is a multiple-database search, which incorporates all of the databases listed below, which contain over 250,000 entries from Belarus.

Enter text to search for in the All Belarus Database,
and choose the type of search:

For "Global Text Search" you can search for single words, or you can search for word combinations using 'AND' and 'OR'.  For example: "TRAUB and Zelman", "KAPLAN or SHAPIRO".

Component Databases:

JewishGen Family Finder
Over 25,000 entries by Jewish genealogists researching families in Belarus.
JewishGen Online Worldwide Burial Registry
Over 8,000 burial records for Belarus and Belarus landsmanshaft cemeteries worldwide.
Grodno Gubernia 1912 Voters List
Names of over 26,000 men of Grodno Gubernia who were eligible to vote in the Russian parliamentary elections in 1912.
Rechitsa Uezd 1906 Voters List
Names of nearly 3,000 men of Rechitsa District (Minsk Gubernia) who were eligible to vote in the Russian Duma elections in 1906.
Minsk Surnames
Index of surnames appearing in the LDS microfilms of the Jewish vital records of the city of Minsk.
Minsk City Homeowners Lists, 1889 and 1911
Nearly 9,000 homeowners from 1889 and 1911 Minsk guidebooks.
Minsk Yizkor Book Index
Over 3,000 names from the index of the two-volume Minsk Yizkor Book Minsk, Ir va-em.
Vsia Rossiia - 1903 & 1911 Minsk and Mogilev Gubernias, 1911 Vitebsk gubernia
Over 12,000 Jewish businesses, from 1903 and 1911 Russian business directories.
Mogilev Birth Index of boys, 1864-1894
Index of over 9,800 male births in Mogilev, listed by father's given name.
1929 Business Directory - Nowogrodek Province
Over 15,000 entries from a Polish business directory.
Pinsk Records from the Soviet Extraordinary Commission
Compilation of testimonials about 11,704 Holocaust victims from Pinsk.
Pinsk Ghetto List, 1942
Data on over 18,000 Jews in the Pinsk ghetto in late 1941 or 1942.
Brest Ghetto Passports
Names and demographics of over 12,000 people in the Brest Ghetto; a database of Holocaust victims drawn from Soviet archives.
Revision Lists  
Over 14,000 records from Reviska Skazka, 19th century Czarist tax censuses - from Minsk, Slutsk and Vileika districts.
Birth Records
Over 6,000 complete birth records, from Kamen, Minsk, Mogilev, Rakov and Rubezhevichi.
Marriage Records
Over 300 complete birth records, from Mogilev.
Yizkor Book Necrologies
22,000 entries from lists of Holocaust martyrs in Yizkor Books for towns in Belarus.
Belarus Names Database
Over 58,000 names that appear on static web pages of the Belarus SIG.
Jewish Religious Personnel in the Russian Empire, 1853-1854
1,900 Jewish religious personnel in Grodno, Minsk, Mogilev, Vilna and Vitebsk Gubernias.
1897 Census for Grodno Gubernia
Detailed family data for 8,000 individuals living in Grodno gubernia, as recorded in the "All Russia Census" of 1897.
