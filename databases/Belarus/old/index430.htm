All Belarus Database

The "All Belarus Database" is a multiple-database search, which incorporates all of the following databases, which contain over 190,000 entries from Belarus.

Brest Ghetto Passports
Names and demographics of over 12,000 people in the Brest Ghetto; a database of Holocaust victims drawn from Soviet archives.
Grodno Gubernia 1912 Voters List
Names of over 26,000 men of Grodno Gubernia who were eligible to vote in the Russian parliamentary elections in 1912.
Rechitsa Uezd 1906 Voters List
Names of nearly 3,000 men of Rechitsa District (Minsk Gubernia) who were eligible to vote in the Russian Duma elections in 1906.
Minsk Surnames
Index of surnames appearing in the LDS microfilms of the Jewish vital records of the city of Minsk.
Vsia Rossiia - 1903 & 1911 Minsk and Mogilev Gubernias, 1911 Vitebsk gubernia
Over 12,000 Jewish businesses, from 1903 and 1911 Russian business directories.
Mogilev Birth Index of boys, 1864-1894
Index of over 9,800 male births in Mogilev, listed by father's given name.
1929 Business Directory - Nowogrodek Province
Over 15,000 entries from a Polish business directory.
Minsk City Homeowners Lists, 1889 and 1911
Nearly 9,000 homeowners from 1889 and 1911 Minsk guidebooks.
Pinsk Ghetto List, 1942
Data on over 18,000 Jews in the Pinsk ghetto in late 1941 or 1942.
Revision Lists
1,000 records from Reviska Skazka, 19th century Czarist tax censuses.
Yizkor Book Necrologies
18,000 entries from lists of Holocaust martyrs in Yizkor Books for towns in Belarus.
Minsk Yizkor Book Index
Over 3,000 names from the index of the two-volume Minsk Yizkor Book Minsk, Ir va-em.
Belarus Names Database
Over 58,000 names that appear on static web pages of the Belarus SIG.
Birth Records
Over 2,500 complete birth records, from Kamen, Minsk, Mogilev, Rakov and Rubezhevichi.
