1869 Hungarian Census — Gazetteer of Szepes Magye

By Marelynn Zipser

This gazetteer of Szepes Magye was compiled for the 1869 Hungarian Census Database Project by Marelynn Zipser.

The 6 districts (járás) of Szepes county (magye) are Banya, Locse, Hernadvolgy, Tatra, Popradvolgy, and Magura.

No data was available for the 13 cities with their own magistrates: Bela, Gnezda, Iglo, Leibicz, Lublo, Podolin, Poprad, Szepes-Olaszi, Szepes-Szombat, Szepes-Varalja, Golnicz, Kesmark, and Locse.  They all had large Jewish populations.
