Tolerated Jews of Pest, Hungary

Background

This list of Tolerated Jews from Pest, Hungary from 1846 includes the Jews who had received official permission to reside and work in Pest. The list was scanned from the archives of the Budapest Jewish Community by Georg Gaugusch. The scanned list, in alphabetical order, can be viewed here.

For further information, please contact: E. Randol Schoenberg.
