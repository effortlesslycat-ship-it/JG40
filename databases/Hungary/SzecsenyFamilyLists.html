Szecseny, Hungary Family Lists

Background

The collection includes late registration of various records from Szecseny and surrounding communities in Nograd megye (county), Hungary for years 1793 to 1894. Includes mohel (circumcision) records and lists of family members including birth dates. Records are from Nograd megye archives. Szecseny, Hungary, is 45 miles NNE of Budapest, and 9 miles NNE of Nyiregyhaza, on the Slovak border.
