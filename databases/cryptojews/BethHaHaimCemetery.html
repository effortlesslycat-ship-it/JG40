Burials in the Beth Hahaim Cemetery in Curacao, Netherland Antilles
Introduction by: Genie Milgrom

Background

This is a partial listing of the families that are buried in this very large cemetery with more than 2,500 tombstones of mostly Spanish Portuguese Sephardim that migrated from Amsterdam to Curacao.

----

Esta es una lista de las familias que están enterradas en este gran cementerio con más de 2500 tombas, en su mayoría sefardíes Portugueses Españoles, que emigraron de Amsterdam a Curazao. El listado muy completo

Database

This database contains 342 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
