CryptoJews: Libro Verde de Aragon - Conversion Data

Background

The Libro Verde De Aragon is the single most important work of Converso Genealogy that was made. In Aragon, this book listed all the names and many genealogies of those that were Condemned by the Inquisition. Their Jewish names are shown and the crimes. Old Spanish families from this region can use this valuable resource. This specific dataset gives us the names and dates of the conversions in the Kingdom of Aragon.Here you can track families who left Aragon for other lands and their previous Spanish names.

Database

This database contains 107 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year of Converstion
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Source
