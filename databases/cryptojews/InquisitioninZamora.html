CryptoJews: Inquisition Cases in the Zamora Region of Spain

Background

This is a list of Inquisition cases from the Zamora region in Spain. It is an area that was near the border with Portugal and many people would be going back and forth.

---

Casos de la Inquisicion de la Region de Zamora en España. Es una zona cerca del borde con Portugal y habia mucho movimiento de un lado al otro.

Database

This database contains 27 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Reference
  • Comments
