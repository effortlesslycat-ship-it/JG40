CryptoJews: Genie Milgrom Personal Research Information
Introduction by: Genie Milgrom

Background

In the many years that I researched my own Crypto Jewish Lineage,  I had to search in archives across Spain and Portugal, Church Archives as well as Inquisition archives and Notary records and last wills and testaments. In this collection you will find the thousands of entries showing each last name only once when in reality it could have been present dozens of times. It was via this search, that I was able to locate my Crypto Jewish ancestors and subsequently my Jewish Lineage

Database

This database contains 1,857 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
