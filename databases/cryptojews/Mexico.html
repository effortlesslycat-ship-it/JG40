CryptoJews: Inquisition of Mexico

Background

Mexico had a long and interesting Inquisition. If you are looking for your ancestry from the United States
or Central America it might be a good place to start with Mexico, you would be able to have a 100 year
head start on your work. This is quite a comprehensive list that mentions the actual record numbers for
the Inquisition trial but you must go back to the archive itself to see more information.

Database

This database contains an index of 2,659 people. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Date
  • Origin
  • Resident
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
