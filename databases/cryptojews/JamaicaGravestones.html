Names from the Gravestones of the Jews of Jamaica
Introduction by: Genie Milgrom

Background

Jamaica has a long and rich Jewish History with Jews coming from all over the Caribbean and England as well as other locations to be able to practice religious freedom. They lived all over the Islands and most had the Spanish -Portuguese last names and customs. The stones were engraved in a myriad of languages and would always give you a clue if they were in English or Spanish or even Portuguese.

There were many small cemeteries all over Jamaica and this list is quite comprehensive.

----

Jamaica tiene una larga y rica historia judía de judíos provenientes de todo el Caribe e Inglaterra, así como de otros paises para poder practicar su libertad religiosa. Vivían por todas partes de las islas y la mayoría tenían apellidos y costumbres Hispano-Portuguesas. Las lapidas estaban grabadas en una gran variedad de idiomas y siempre te daran una pista si estaban en inglés, Español o incluso Portugués.

Habían muchos cementerios pequeños en todo Jamaica y esta lista es bastante completa.

 

Database

This database contains 1,431 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
