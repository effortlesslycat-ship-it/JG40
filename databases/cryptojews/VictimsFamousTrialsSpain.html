CryptoJews: Victims of Famous Trials in Spain

Background

This is a selection of Victims from Famous Trials in Spain. They are from different cities and most have caught the attention of academics and dissertations. They were unusual either because of the length of the trial, the amount of accusations made or because they caught the attention of the Investigator.

---

Esta es una seleccion de victimas de algunos Casos famosos de la Inquisicion. Estos Procesos son de diferentes ciudades y por algun motivo, fueron de interes para los academicos. A veces por que era diferente, a veces por que mencionaba muchos acusados pero por un motivo u otro, llamo la atencion.

Database

This database contains 168 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
