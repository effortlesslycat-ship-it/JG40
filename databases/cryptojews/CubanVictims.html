CryptoJews: Cuban Victims of the Inquisicion

Background

My colleague, Cuban Born Investigator, Eugenio Alonso, has been researching the Crypto Jews from the Inquisition in Cuba and the Cuban born in Inquisitions in other locations. In this collection, he has managed to consolidate what is known about those sentenced by the Inquisition on the Island.

---

Mi colega, Eugenio Alonso, un investigador nacido en Cuba , lleva tiempo en los archivos buscando todo lo relacionado con los victimas de la Inqusicion en Cuba asi como aquellos Cubanos que los procesaron en Tribunales de otras partes del mundo. Un excelente trabajo !

Database

This database contains 46 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Year
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
