CryptoJews: Inquisition of Sevilla

Background

This is a partial list of those that were caught in The Tribunal of the Inquisition in Sevilla Spain. The reason that these files are incomplete is that all of this information has not been digitized and therefore must be taken from books or other available registers. Authors of a particular did not usually cite all those that were arrested.

---

Esta es una lista parcial de algunos de los que fueron prendidos en El Tribunal de La Inquisicion de Sevilla. El motivo que esta lista esta incompleta es que hay que tomar la informacion de diferentes autores ya que no estan completamente digitados los expedientes. Los Autores de estos regitros muchas veces no tenian todo includio.

Database

This database contains an index of 218 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Auto Da Fe Date
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Crime/Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
