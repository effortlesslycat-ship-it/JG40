Background

This partial listing of the Cartagena, Colombia Inquisition shows us that many were sentenced for
Herbalist, palm readers and witchcraft. These were most likely Jews that no specific crime could be
found for. Take these” crimes” with a grain of salt because anything would be used to bring people
before The Inquisition Tribunal.
