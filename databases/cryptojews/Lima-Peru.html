CryptoJews: Inquisition of Limu, Peru

Background

The Inquisition of Lima Files are some of the most important ones because this Tribunal was responsible for a large portion of South America. The problem is that they have not been Digitized nor indexed. Many are missing yet I was told in person that the collection contains 70 Boxes.
The information here comes from one book that was written while sitting inside the National Library in the late 1800s and transcribed. That is how we come to the information. I do not feel it is complete and every author usually includes only what is available at the time.

Biblioteca Nacional a fines del siglo XIX y ahi transcribió lo que habia disponible. Así es como llegamos a la información. No creo que esté completo y cada autor generalmente incluye solo lo que está disponible en ese momento.

Database

This database contains an index of 560 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Auto Date
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Crime/Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
