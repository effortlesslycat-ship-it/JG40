CryptoJews: Inquisition cases from Torre de Tombo archives pertaining to Covilha in Portugal
Introduction by: Genie Milgrom

Background

These cases were compiled from the Inquisition Records available at the Torre Do Tombo Archives in Lisbon. The cases listed are either from the Town of Covilha, or have relatives from there. It would be unusual that the collection is fully complete so there may be some gaps. It first appeared on the e mail magazine of Saudades and is still available on the reference shown in the tables.

--

Casos recopilados de los archivos de Torre Do Tombo cubriendo todos los que pasaron la Inquisicion que fueron de origen de Covilha en Portugal o algun familiar o esposo tenia este origen. Aun que siempre se trata de que las colecciones especificas esten completes, no siempre es possible. Aqui esta la cantidad que primero aparecio en la revista e mail of Saudades y todavia aparece complete en la referencia en la Tabla .

Database

This database contains 381 records. Most of the records include the following fields:

  • Last Name
  • Alias
  • Date
  • Origin
  • Residence
  • Occupation
  • Sentence
  • Comments
  • Reference
