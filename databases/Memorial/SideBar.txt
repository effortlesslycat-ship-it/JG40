The <A HREF="/databases/Memorial" STYLE="text-decoration: none"><B>JewishGen 
Memorial Plaques Database</B></A> 
aims to index information from synagogue yahrzeit plaques 
and other memorial records.&nbsp;  
The JewishGen Memorial Plaques Database is a 
<A HREF="/databases/Memorial">searchable database</A>
of names and associated information.&nbsp;  
As of December 2021, the JewishGen Memorial Plaques Database 
contains 225,000 memorial records from
<A HREF="/databases/Memorial/tree/MemList.htm">417 Synagogues</A>.
