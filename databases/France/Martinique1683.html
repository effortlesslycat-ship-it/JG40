Jews Living in Martinique, 1683

Introduction By Eric Feinstein

BACKGROUND

This is a list found in the Les Archives nationales d'outre-mer (ANOM) the French Archives of the former overseas territorial possessions of France. 5 DPPC 53 -liste des juifs Útablis en Martinique, DÚnombrement des juifs demeurant en l'¯le de la Martinique (n░ 44)

DATABASE

This database includes 225 Jews that served in Martinque in 1683 as well as the slaves that made up their respective households.

The fields in the database are:

  • Status
  • Surname
  • Given name
  • Other surnames
  • Age
  • Spouse's name
  • Father's name
  • Mother's name
  • Comments
