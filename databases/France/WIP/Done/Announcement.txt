JewishGen is pleased to announce the "JewishGen France Database",
a multi-database search facility containing more than 150,000 records 
of Jews living in France and other French-speaking regions.  
It is JewishGen's 14th "All Country" database.


The "JewishGen France Database" searches all of JewishGen's records
for France, other French-speaking areas such as Belgium, Luxembourg 
and Switzerland, and former French colonies and protectorates such as 
Tunisia, Algeria, and Morocco.  The database is a work in progress, 
and new entries will being added regularly. 

The "JewishGen France Database" is located at:
http://www.jewishgen.org/databases/France


Components of the "JewishGen France Database" include:

* Jewish Marriages in Alsace, 1792-1899 --  
Records of 22,000 Jewish marriages in the dťpartements of Bas-Rhin 
and Haut-Rhin, compiled by Pierre Hahn.

* The JewishGen Online Worldwide Burial Registry (JOWBR) --
70,000 records of Jewish burials in cemeteries in France and 
French-speaking regions:
    + France     (643 cemeteries, 42,631 burials)
    + Belgium     (72 cemeteries,  4,535 burials)	
    + Luxembourg   (4 cemeteries,    315 burials)	
    + Switzerland (23 cemeteries, 15,554 burials)
    + Algeria     (30 cemeteries,  4,717 burials)      
    + Morocco     (22 cemeteries,  8,277 burials)      
    + Tunisia     (10 cemeteries,    183 burials)      

* The JewishGen Family Finder (JGFF) --
More than 14,000 surnames and towns of Jewish families
of interest to genealogists researching French Jewry.

* The JewishGen Memorial Plaques Database --
Information from synagogue yahrzeit plaques.

* Component datsets of the "JewishGen Holocaust Database" which
relate to French Jewry, more than 40,000 records, including:
    + French Jews who were issued immigration visas to Brazil 
    + French Prisoners in the Stutthof concentration camp
    + "Certificates of Disappearance" for French female deportees 
    + Hidden Children in France
    + Gurs Deportations to Auschwitz-Birkenau
    + Rivesaltes Deportations to Auschwitz-Birkenau
    + Jewish Arrivals in Switzerland, 1938-1945


The "JewishGen France Database" can be found at:
http://www.jewishgen.org/databases/France
