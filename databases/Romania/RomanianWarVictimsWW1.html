Background

This database is a list of 881 Romanian Jewish soldiers who were killed or missing in action during World War I. The list was published in two issues of Rom-SIG News: Volume 6, No. 2 (Winter 1997–1998) and Volume 6, No. 3 (Spring 1998).

The list was taken from Evreii Din Romania in Razboiul de reintregire a tarii 1916–1919 [Jews in Romania during the war of the integration of the country 1916–1919], edited by Dumitru Hincu and Lya Benjamin (Bucuresti: Editura Hasefer, 1996), and was reprinted by permission of the publisher. The collection was discovered and photographed in Romania by Rick Bercuvitz, and transcribed for this index by Carolyn Halbert.

Source: Evreii Din Romania in Razboiul de reintregire a tarii 1916–1919, Dumitru Hincu and Lya Benjamin, eds. Bucuresti: Editura Hasefer, 1996. Originally reprinted in Rom-SIG News, Vol. 6, Nos. 2–3 (1997–1998).
