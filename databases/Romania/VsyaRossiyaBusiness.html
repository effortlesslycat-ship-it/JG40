Vsya Rossiya Business Directory for Bessarabia

4,014 Records

Business Directory · Bessarabia · Transnistria · Moldova · Ukraine

Background

This database contains nearly 4,000 entries of apparently Jewish names from editions of the Russian business directory Vsya Rossiya, for Bessarabia and Transnistria from the period 1897 through 1912. Vsya Rossiya (“All Russia”) was a series of directories published yearly by Aleksei Sergeevich Suvorin from 1895 through 1923, including detailed lists of government offices, public services, and medium and large businesses.

The database includes entries for Bessarabia gubernia from all directories, plus entries for portions of Kherson and Podolia gubernias now in Transnistria (from all directories except 1897). Among the most common businesses were bakeries, books, bread-grain, drug stores, groceries, haberdashery, ironware, leather, ready-to-wear clothing, textile merchandise, timber, and tobacco. Surnames that did not appear Jewish were omitted, erring on the side of inclusion.
