Background

This list of Sephardic Jewish surnames involved in Romania’s economic life was kindly provided by Lea Haber Gedalia in Israel. It is a compilation of Sephardic surnames from an article by Yaakov Geller, Hayehudim hasefaradim b’chyei hacalcala shel Romania (in Hebrew), published in Pe’amim, 1987.

Acknowledgements

We acknowledge the tremendous contributions and lifelong dedication of Mathilde Tagger, z”l, who made this index available. For many years, and right until her untimely death, Mathilde Tagger was a close friend and collaborator with Jeff Malka. Together they worked to promote Sephardic genealogy research and educate the public about its enormous potential.

We also express our grateful appreciation to Dr. Jeff Malka for his monumental ongoing effort to collect and make accessible Sephardic genealogical information, and for his generosity in contributing his extraordinarily valuable collection to JewishGen.
