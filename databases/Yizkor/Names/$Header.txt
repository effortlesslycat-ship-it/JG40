<A NAME="Intro"></A>
<P>The <B>JewishGen Yizkor Book Master Name Index</B> 
(&ldquo;YBMNI&rdquo;) is a master index of all persons mentioned in 
the translated portions of the Yizkor Books on the 
<A HREF="/Yizkor/translations.html">JewishGen Yizkor Book Project</A> 
website.&nbsp;    
This database is only an index of names; it directs researchers back 
to the Yizkor Book itself, where more information may be available.
</P>

<P>This database allows the surnames to be searched phonetically and 
via <A HREF="/InfoFiles/Soundex.html">soundex</A>.&nbsp; 
Because most of these names were transliterated from Hebrew and Yiddish, 
the spellings of the surnames may not be as you are used to seeing them 
in Latin-alphabet sources.
</P>
