South African Jewry 1976-1977

Introduction By Eric Feinstein

BACKGROUND

South African Jewry is one of the largest Jewish communities on the African continent. This collection comprises a list of Jewish persons in South African Jewry 1976-1977, Third Edition. A Survey of the Jewish Community; Its Contributions to South Africa; Directory of Communal Institutions; and a Who's Who of Leading Personalities, edited by Leon Feldberg. There are 2,176 biographies, with most providing additional names such as spouses and parents

DATABASE

This database includes 2176 Jews listed in a South African directory from 1976-1977.

The fields in the database are:

  • Surname
  • Given name(s)
  • Date and Place of Birth
  • Father's Name
  • Mother's Name
  • Spouse's Name
  • Date of Marriage
  • Date and Place of Death
