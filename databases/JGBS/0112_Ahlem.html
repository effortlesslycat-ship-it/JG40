Background

This collection consists of the names of 257 prisoners, mostly apparently Jews, who perished in a subcamp of concentration camp Neuengamme located in Ahlem/Hannover.  These prisoners, mostly Polish, had been sent there from Auschwitz to work in a rubber factory.  The story of the prisoners at this factory is described in Wir wollten Gef³hle sichtbar werden lassen, a book by Janet Ansch³tz and Irmtraud Heike, Edition Temmen, 2004.  The list was provided by the Neuengamme Memorial Site.

Database

The database includes 257 individuals.

The fields of the database are as follows:

  • Surname
  • Given Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Country of Birth
  • Date of Death
  • Place of Death
  • Occupation
  • Prisoner Number
