Background

This database includes 1,761 records of Jewish prisoners from the Wezienie Karne Warszawa Mokotow prison, in Warsaw.

This 20 reel collection contains a list of Jewish prisoners held at Wezienie Karne Warszawa Mokotow prison, in Warsaw, between 1939 and 1942.  While the list only provides the name of a prisoner, there is an extensive file giving additional information on each person and his/her criminal charges such as robbery, rape, theft, etc. with a copy of the indictment and verdict.  The records on non-Jews at this prison were not filmed.

According to the USHMM, "The collection contains administrative and prisoners' personal files. The prisoners' files relate to such crimes as: espionage, contraband, theft, forgery, robbery, rape, arson, murder, illegal trade, and lack of subordination to authority.  Particular files contain documents with significant details concerning the prisoner such as the reason for imprisonment, copies of the accusation [indictment] and verdict, the prisoners' health, certificates of death, correspondence, etc."

Furthermore, "The collection "Wiezienie Karne Warszawa Mokotów" is the only well preserved collection of this type held in the Polish State Archives.

Database

This database includes 1,761 records of Jewish prisoners from the Wezienie Karne Warszawa Mokotow prison, in Warsaw. The fields for this database are as follows:

  • File #
  • Surname
  • Given Name

Acknowledgments

The information contained in this database was indexed from the files of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM RG 15.113M).  Copies of individual files may be requested from the United States Holocaust Museum Archives.
