This database is a list of 1,226 Theresienstadt survivors who were sent to Canton St. Gallen in Switzerland in February 1945.

Background

This collection includes Theresienstadt survivors sent to Canton St. Gallen in Switzerland in February 1945.  While the collection contains no explanation, it is assumed that these persons were one of several groups of survivors sent to Switzerland near and after the end of the war for recovery and medical treatment.  NoĀinformation on the further fate/transfer of these survivors is given.

The information consists of name, nationality, date of birth and facility in St. Gallen where the individuals were housed.  The four facilities were Les Avants, Adliswil, Tour Haldimant and Kant.Spital (Cantonal hospital).

Database

This database includes 1,226 records of survivors sent to Canton St.Gallen.  The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Name (Surname + Given Name)
  • Maiden name
  • Nationality
  • Location
  • Date of birth

Acknowledgments

The information contained in this database was indexed from the files of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM), RG 14.053M, Reel 1.  The original source material was filmed at the Jewish Community in Stuttgart, Germany.  Freija Lindholm, a JewishGen volunteer, transcribed the list.
