Claims Conference, Hungary - Source List

The Claims Conference - Hungary Database contains records of 135,544 Hungarian Jews whose names appear in records held in the Central Zionist Archives and Yad Vashem in Israel.

The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM) has the finding aids for the original source material underlying this database. The finding aids consist of two files listing the sources in the Central Zionist Archives (CZA) and in Yad Vashem (YVS).
