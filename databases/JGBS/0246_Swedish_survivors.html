Background

The Holocaust Memorial in Stockholm, Sweden includes the engraved in stone names of over 8,000 Jews who perished in the Holocaust, including their years and places of birth and death.  Many of these names are available from other sources.  However, what is unique is that the Memorial also includes the names of 283 Jewish Holocaust survivors who came to Sweden at the end of World War II but perished there in 1945-46.

This list gives names, years and places of birth and dates and places of death in Sweden.  The list also appears in a Swedish-language book, Glöm oss inte: en bok om Förintelsen och minnesmonumentet i Stockholm ("Forget us Not"), published in Stockholm by Hillelförlaget in 1999.  139 pages. [OCLC #42249078].

More information on many of these persons is available in the International Tracing Service (ITS) collection, which can be accessed in Bad Arolsen, Washington, Jerusalem, London and Paris.

Database

This database contains 282 records of Holocaust survivors who died in Sweden.  The fields for this database are as follows:

  • Name (Surname + Given name)
  • Year of Birth
  • Place of Birth
  • Year of Death
  • Place of Death (i.e. a city in Sweden)
  • Comments

Acknowledgments

The information contained in this database was indexed from the book, Glöm oss inte: en bok om Förintelsen och minnesmonumentet i Stockholm ("Forget us Not"), published in Stockholm in 1999.
