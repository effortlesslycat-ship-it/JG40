The Jewish Doctors of Egypt

Introduction By Eric Feinstein

BACKGROUND

This is a list of Jewish doctors that lived in Egypt in the 1930s. It was compiled at the end of the book Medecins Juifs D’Egypte. This was part of the book Medecins et Avocats Juifs Au Service De L’Egypte by Maurice Fargeon published in Cairo (Le Caire) by Imprimerie Lencioni in 1939. The book contains a history of Jewish involvement in the medical field from Roman times to the Rambam and then the present day (1939.) The book was digitized by the Centre d’Etudes Alexandrines

DATABASE

This database includes 48 Jewish Doctors practicing medicine in Egypt in 1939.

The fields in the database are:

  • Surname
  • Given name
  • Type of doctor
  • City of practice
  • Date of birth
  • Place of birth
  • Father's name
  • Year of arrival in Egypt
  • Comments
