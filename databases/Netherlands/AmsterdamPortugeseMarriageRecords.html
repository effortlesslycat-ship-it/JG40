Background

This database is an index to more than 4,000 “marriage intention” notices, known as Banns, of Sephardic Jews in Amsterdam, spanning the years 1598–1811. The banns record a couple’s intent to marry, together with the names of witnesses and, frequently, the birth years and origins of the bride and groom.

The information was originally made available by Akevoth via dutchjewry.org, and added to JewishGen to improve its searchability.

Fields Recorded
Groom — first name, patronym, last name, complete name, and birth year
Groom’s Origin — place of origin or residence
Groom’s Witness — first name, patronym, last name, complete name, and family relation
Bride — first name, patronym, last name, complete name, and birth year
Bride’s Witness — first name, complete name, and family relation
Hebrew Signature — where recorded
Marriage Year & Marriage Account Number
