<h1 align="CENTER">The Jeff Malka Sephardic Collection</h1>
<h2 style="text-align: center;"><em><strong><span>Tombstone Inscriptions of Aleppo Rabbis</span></strong></em></h2>
<p><span></span></p>
<p><span>This list is the transcription and translation of tombstone inscriptions of 250 rabbis from Aleppo most of whom were buried there, as reported in the Hebrew book </span><em>Sefer Asher LeKedoshim beAram Tsoba</em><book for="" the="" holy="" men="" in="" aleppo="">, by Rabbi David Tsion Laniado, published in Jerusalem in 1952.</book></p>
<p><book for="" the="" holy="" men="" in="" aleppo="">The present index provides the surname, the given name and the father's name - when available - as well as the Hebrew year of death, the corresponding Gregorian year, the place of death and notes.</book></p>
<h2>Acknowledgements</h2>
<p><span>We acknowledge the tremendous contributions and lifelong dedication of Mathilde Tagger, z'l, who made this index available. In addition, we express our grateful appreciation to </span><b>Dr. Jaff Malka </b>for his monumental ongoing effort to collect and make accessible Sephardic genealogical information, and for his generosity in contributing his extraordinarily valuable collection to JewishGen.</p>
