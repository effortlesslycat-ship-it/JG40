<P>This is a database of Jews from the town of Oshpitzin 
(Polish: O&#347;wi&#281;cim [Oshvyentzim], German: Auschwitz).&nbsp;
The information has been compiled from 
<A HREF="/yizkor/oswiecim1/oswiecim.html"><I>Sefer Oshpitsin</I></A>, 
the Oswiecim memorial book, and from other sources.&nbsp; 
The database attempts to include all individuals who were either 
born in Oshpitzin and surrounding townlets, or lived there for a 
period between 1919 and 1941.&nbsp;  There are 5,511 entries. </P>

<P>Here is a description of the fields contained in this database:

<UL>
<LI>Family name + first name(s)
<LI>Maiden surname
<LI>Date of birth or Age
<LI>Status:
  <UL>
  <LI>HY'D = Holocaust victim &nbsp; ("Hashem Yikom Damam" = "May G-d avenge their blood") 
  <LI>N'Y  = Living at time of submission of memorial &nbsp; ("Nero Ya'ir" = "May their light shine")
  <LI>Z'L  = Died before or after the war &nbsp; ("Zichrono Livracha" = "of blessed memory")
  <LI>P'V  = Presumed victim
  <LI>UND  = Undetermined - possible duplicate
  <LI>MZV  = Matzeva - tombstone at the Oshpitzin cemetery
  <LI>RHS  = appears in Registry of Jewish Holocaust Survivors (United States Holocaust Memorial Museum, 1996)
  </UL>
<LI>Father
<LI>Mother + mother's maiden surname
<LI>Spouse + spouse's maiden surname

<LI>Sources:
  <UL>
  <LI>HoN  = Hall of Names - <A HREF="http://www.yad-vashem.org.il">Yad Vashem</A>
             Page of Testimony (when doubled, indicates two sources with additional 
             or differing details)
  <LI>Y568 = Mentioned in <A HREF="/yizkor/oswiecim1/oswiecim.html"><I>Sefer
             Oshpitsin</I></A> Yizkor Book, on page 568.
  <LI>SKL  = Listed in Serge Klarsfeld's Deportations List - 
             <A HREF="/InfoFiles/FR-KLARS.txt"><I>Le memorial de la deportation 
             des Juifs de France</I></A>.
  <LI>JS   = Added by Joseph Schachter from personal knowledge
  <LI>SWD  = Swedish Yizkor book by survivors - <I>Gloem Oss Inte</I> 
             (Stockholm: Hillelforlaget, 1999)
  <LI>TRZ  = Theresienstadt Lists - <I>Terezinska Pametni Kniha</I>, (Praha, 1995).
  <LI>OGS  = Oorlogsgravenstichting - Dutch deportations lists
  </UL>

<LI>Photograph - A photograph exists at Yad Vashem's Photo Archives.
  <UL>
  <LI>A120, denotes 120th in Szrodula Ghetto list for the initial letter A.<BR>
     (Photos were taken in 1942-3 and signed by the victims)
  </UL>
<LI>Remarks - Added information about individual or about family members.  
    Not all were killed, nor were all from Oshpitzin.
<LI>Reported by - There are often more sources, but only one is given here.
<LI>Birthplace
<LI>Residence pre 1939
<LI>Lived - Where survivor or former Oshpitziner lived before or after the War.
<LI>War Years - Only one of several possibilities. Most lived in Oshpitzin 
    until expulsion to Szrodula or Chrzanow in 1941.  
    By the end of 1943 the ghettoes were "liquidated".
<LI>Death date
<LI>Death details - KZ = Kazet = Concentration camp - forced labor
</UL>
</P>

<P>If you discover any errors in this database or have additional information 
or totally new entries please contact
