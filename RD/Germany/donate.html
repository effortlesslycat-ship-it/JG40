About the Division

The JewishGen German Research Division (RD), formerly GerSIG, is a group of researchers dedicated to learning more about the lives of our Jewish ancestors who lived in current and former German-speaking regions of Europe. This includes not only modern-day Germany, but also regions that are today part of Poland and France.

If you would be interested in making a major contribution to the Germany Research Division's efforts, please email Development@mjhnyc.org.

Support the Germany Research Division

Donor support enables the Division to acquire new materials, index rare records, and make them widely accessible for future generations.
