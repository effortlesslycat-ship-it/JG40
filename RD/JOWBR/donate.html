What Is JOWBR?

The JewishGen Online Worldwide Burial Registry is the world's largest centralized database of Jewish burial records — a permanent digital archive of headstone inscriptions, cemetery registers, and historical burial data from every corner of the globe. Records are freely searchable by anyone and updated twice annually.

Support JOWBR

Donor support enables us to preserve and document Jewish cemeteries around the world, ensuring the history and memory of our communities is maintained.
