About the Division

The French Research Division, formerly French-SIG, focuses on Jewish genealogical research in France and French colonies, as well as other French-speaking areas such as Belgium, Luxembourg and Switzerland.

If you would be interested in making a major contribution to the French Research Division's efforts, please email Development@mjhnyc.org.

Support the France Research Division

Donor support enables the Division to acquire new materials, index rare records, and make them widely accessible for future generations.
