Ukraine Research Division

Preserving and sharing the histories of Jewish communities from the former Russian Empire gubernias now in Ukraine: Podolia, Volhynia, Kiev, Poltava, Chernigov, Kharkov, Kherson, Taurida and Ekaterinoslav.

About the Division

Welcome to the JewishGen Ukraine Research Division (RD). We are so glad that you are here! We look forward to helping researchers — those at the beginning of their Jewish genealogy journey as well as those who have been working on their Jewish family tree for decades — to discover information about Jewish ancestors who lived in Ukraine.

For donations of $100 (plus transaction fees) or more to JewishGen's Ukraine Documents Acquisition and Transcription Project, you can choose two Ukrainian towns for priority transcription of irreplaceable Jewish records. Email your two town selections to pberenson@jewishgen.org.

If you would be interested in making a major contribution to the Ukraine Research Division's efforts, please email Development@mjhnyc.org.
