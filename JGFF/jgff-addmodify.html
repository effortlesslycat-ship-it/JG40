Instructions & formatting rules ▼

Each entry consists of one surname associated with one town within one country. Enter only one surname per record.

Cohen Warszawa Poland <-- CORRECT Kahn Warszawa Poland <-- CORRECT Cohen/Kahn Warszawa Poland <-- INCORRECT
Surname spelling
You do not need to enter variant spellings. The Daitch-Mokotoff Soundex search finds all variants automatically.
Town names
Use the modern contemporary native spelling. For example: Warszawa not Warsaw; Vilnius not Vilna; Wien not Vienna.
USA & Canada
Include the two-letter state or province abbreviation as part of the town name: New York, NY or Toronto, ON.
Country names
Enter the country in which the town is located today, regardless of historical borders.
Unknown town
If you know the country but not the specific town, use Any in the Town field (e.g. Any, Poland). Use sparingly.
Characters
Use only the 26 Latin letters. No slashes, dashes, hyphens, parentheses, brackets, numbers, or accented characters. Use spaces for hyphens: Frankfurt am Main.
