About the JGFF

The JGFF is indexed and cross-referenced by both surname and town name, making it easy to find researchers with overlapping interests. All Jewish genealogists are encouraged to participate by adding their own ancestral surnames and towns.

For complete information about the database, including instructions for searching and adding entries, see the JGFF Frequently Asked Questions.
