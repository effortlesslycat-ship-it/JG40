JGFF Help Desk

Before writing to the JGFF Help Desk, please read the JGFF FAQ — Frequently Asked Questions About the JewishGen Family Finder to see if your question is already answered there.
Passwords

Forgotten password

If you have forgotten your JewishGen password, use the password reset page to create a new one. No need to contact the Help Desk for this.

Town Names

Questionable town entry

The JGFF accepts only modern native town names and will reject entries that have not been verified in the system. If your town entry was rejected, read the error message carefully and then review JGFF FAQ Question 4.2 to confirm you are using the correct modern spelling.

If you believe your town entry should be accepted, submit the Town Query Form and the JGFF editor will review it.

Other Questions

All other technical questions

If your question is not answered in the JGFF FAQ, contact the Help Desk at jgffhelp@jewishgen.org.

When writing, tell us exactly what the problem is. Don’t tell us “it doesn’t work” — that gives us nothing to go on. Describe what you were trying to do, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened. Include the exact text of any error message you received.

Every message to the Help Desk should include:

  • Your full name and email address.
  • The URL of the page where the problem occurred (the address in your browser starting with “https://”).
  • The browser you are using (e.g., Chrome 124, Firefox 125, Safari 17, Edge 124).
  • Your operating system (e.g., Windows 11, macOS 14, iOS 17, Android 14).
  • The date, time, and time zone when the problem occurred.

Please write in complete sentences and establish context. If we cannot understand the message, we cannot help you.

Email the JGFF Help Desk
