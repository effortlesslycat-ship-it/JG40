About the Yizkor Book Project

A JewishGen project translating and indexing the Yizkor books of Eastern and Central Europe.

Yizkor (memorial) books were written by survivors and former residents to preserve the Jewish communities of Eastern and Central Europe lost in the Holocaust. The Yizkor Book Project translates them so these stories reach as many readers as possible.
