Locating a Jewish Community

Database

JewishGen Communities Database →

Soundex searching of the U.S. Board of Geographic Names database of central and eastern European communities. Gives current name, latitude and longitude, and map location.

 Book

Where Once We Walked →

A Guide to the Jewish Communities Destroyed in the Holocaust. Revised Edition, by Gary Mokotoff and Sallyann Amdur Sack with Alexander Sharon (Bergenfield, NJ: Avotaynu, 2002). Locates 23,500 towns in Central and Eastern Europe where Jews resided.
Book

Historical Atlas of East Central Europe

By Paul Robert Magocsi (Seattle & London: Univ. of Washington Press, 1993). Describes historical boundary changes for countries and administrative districts in East Central Europe.
