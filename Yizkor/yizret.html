Retail Establishments with Yizkor Book Holdings

A JewishGen InfoFile

This file lists retail establishments, in the United States and abroad, that have substantial inventories of Yizkor books. Because of the constantly changing nature of book inventories, the extent of individual holdings is not shown.

No member of the Yizkor Book committee that assembled this list has a commercial interest in any of the establishments listed below. This list is published solely as an aid for those researching Jewish communities for genealogical purposes; a listing does not constitute an endorsement.
